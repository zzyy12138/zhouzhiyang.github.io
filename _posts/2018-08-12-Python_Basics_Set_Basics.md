### 创建与去重

>```python
>s = set([1, 2, 2, 3])  # {1, 2, 3}
>```

### 常见运算

>```python
>a, b = {1, 2, 3}, {3, 4}
>print(a | b, a & b, a - b, a ^ b)
>```

### 不可变集合

>```python
>fs = frozenset([1, 2, 3])
># fs.add(4)  # AttributeError
>```
